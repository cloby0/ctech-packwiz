In this pack, the following languages are available:

- English (en_us)
- European French (fr_fr)
- Canadian French (fr_ca)
- Simplified Chinese (zh_cn)

Unfortunately, the translations from English are NOT complete. If you would like to contribute translations for any of the existing
languages or an entirely new language, please contact me over Discord from the Point Blank server (my handle is thescepticblock).

I will make sure to credit you for your translations if you do contribute.
